Neapolitan Pizza

Ingredients

  • 250g neapolitan
  • 40g butter
  • 40g plain flour
  • 600ml milk
  • 250g cheddar, grated
  • 50g Parmesan (or a similar), finely grated

Instructions

  1. Cook the neapolitan in a large saucepan of boiling salted water for 8–10 minutes. Drain well and set aside.
  2. Melt the butter over a medium heat in a saucepan slightly larger than that used for the neapolitan. Add the flour and stir to form a roux, cooking for a few minutes.
  3. Gradually whisk in the milk, a little at a time. Cook for 10–15 minutes to a thickened and smooth sauce.
  4. Preheat the grill to hot.
  5. Remove the sauce from the hob, add 175g/6oz of the cheddar and stir until it is well combined and melted.
  6. Add the neapolitan to the sauce and mix well. Transfer to a deep suitably-sized ovenproof dish.
  7. Sprinkle over the remaining cheddar and the Parmesan and place the dish under the hot grill. Cook until the cheese is browned and bubbling. Serve straightaway.

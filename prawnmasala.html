Prawn Masala

MAIN IMAGE PLACEHOLDER

Ingredients

  • 200g prawns, cleaned
  • Tomato, onion, spices, garlic, ginger

Instructions

  1. Sauté onions, garlic, ginger.
  2. Add prawns and cook briefly.
  3. Mix in spices and tomato, simmer.
  4. Serve with rice or bread.

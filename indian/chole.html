Chole (Chickpea Curry)

MAIN IMAGE PLACEHOLDER

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas (or 2 cans cooked chickpeas)
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • Spices: cumin seeds, garam masala, turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. If using dried chickpeas, soak overnight, then boil until soft. If canned, drain and rinse.
  2. Heat oil in a pot, add cumin seeds, and let them sizzle.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden.
  4. Stir in garlic and ginger, cook for a minute.
  5. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  6. Mix in spices and cook for another minute.
  7. Add chickpeas and some water if needed. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  8. Adjust salt and spice levels. Garnish with cilantro if desired.

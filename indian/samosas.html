Samosas

MAIN IMAGE PLACEHOLDER

Ingredients

  • Spring roll or samosa wrappers
  • Potatoes, peas, spices
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Prepare spiced potato filling.
  2. Fill wrappers with the mixture.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Serve hot with chutney.

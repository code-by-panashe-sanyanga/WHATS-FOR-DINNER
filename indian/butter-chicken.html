Butter Chicken

MAIN IMAGE PLACEHOLDER

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breasts or thighs, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Spices: garam masala, turmeric, cumin, chili powder

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices.
  2. Sauté butter and spices, then add tomato puree.
  3. Add chicken and cook until tender.
  4. Finish with cream or butter, and serve.

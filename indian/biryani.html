Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb chicken, lamb, or vegetables
  • Spices: saffron, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves
  • Yogurt, onions, garlic, ginger

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice with spices.
  2. Prepare the meat or vegetables with spices and yogurt.
  3. Layer rice and cooked meat/vegetables, then steam together.
  4. Serve hot with raita or salad.

Chicken Teriyaki

Ingredients

  • 650 g Chicken breasts, Skinless, boneless, cut into chunks
  • 1 tbsp Oil, Sesame, vegetable or sunflower
  • 2 Cloves of garlic, Peeled and crushed
  • 1 tsp Ginger puree
  • 2 tsp Cornflour
  • 100 ml Soy sauce
  • 50 ml Water
  • 3 tbsp Honey
  • 0.5 tsp Garlic powder
  • 1 tsp Ginger purée
  • 0.5 tsp Dried chilli flakes
  • Sesame Seeds

Instructions

  1. Mix the teriyaki sauce ingredients in a small bowl. Begin with the cornflour and gradually add the soy sauce, whisking so there are no lumps. Add water, honey, garlic powder, ginger puree and chilli flakes.
  2. Heat the oil in a frying pan until very hot.
  3. Add the chicken and cook for about 10-15 minutes until the chicken is almost cooked through.
  4. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for 3 or 4 more minutes until softened.
  5. Add the teriyaki sauce ingredients and cook for about 5 minutes until everything is well coated and thickened.
  6. While the chicken is cooking, I cook some rice in the bottom of a steamer pan and then add some green vegetables to the steamer part on top for the last 5 minutes.

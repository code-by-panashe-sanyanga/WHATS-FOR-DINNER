Sweet and Sour Pork

MAIN IMAGE PLACEHOLDER

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork loin, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks
  • For the sauce: vinegar, ketchup, soy sauce, sugar

Instructions

  1. Fry the pork until crispy and set aside.
  2. Prepare the sweet and sour sauce in a separate pan.
  3. Add fried pork, bell peppers, and pineapple to the sauce.
  4. Cook until heated through and serve.

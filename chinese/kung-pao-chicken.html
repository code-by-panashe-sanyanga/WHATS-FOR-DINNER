Kung Pao Chicken

MAIN IMAGE PLACEHOLDER

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, diced
  • 1/2 cup peanuts
  • Chili peppers, chopped
  • Soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, sugar

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in soy sauce.
  2. Stir-fry chicken until cooked.
  3. Add chili peppers and peanuts.
  4. Mix in sauces and cook a few more minutes.

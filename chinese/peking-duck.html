Peking Duck

MAIN IMAGE PLACEHOLDER

Ingredients

  • Whole duck
  • Hoisin sauce, scallions, thin pancakes

Instructions

  1. Prepare and roast the duck until crispy.
  2. Slice the duck meat.
  3. Serve with pancakes, hoisin sauce, and scallions.

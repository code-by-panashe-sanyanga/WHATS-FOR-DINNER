Sichuan Hot Pot

MAIN IMAGE PLACEHOLDER

Ingredients

  • Sichuan peppercorns
  • Chili oil
  • Assorted meats and vegetables
  • Broth base (chicken or beef)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the spicy broth with Sichuan spices.
  2. Bring broth to a boil in a hot pot.
  3. Add meats and vegetables to cook at the table.
  4. Serve with dipping sauces.

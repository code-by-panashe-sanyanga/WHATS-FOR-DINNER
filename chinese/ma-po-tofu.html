Ma Po Tofu

MAIN IMAGE PLACEHOLDER

Ingredients

  • 1 block soft tofu
  • Minced pork (optional)
  • Sichuan peppercorns, chili bean paste, garlic, ginger

Instructions

  1. Sauté garlic and ginger.
  2. Add minced pork and cook through.
  3. Stir in chili bean paste and spices.
  4. Add tofu and simmer for a few minutes.

Churros

40 MINS PREP SERVES 4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • Vegetable oil (for deep-frying)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • Cinnamon (to taste)

Instructions

01

Prep and Heat: Sift the flour into a medium bowl. Mix the sugar and cinnamon in a shallow dish for coating. Heat 1 inch of oil in a wide heavy pan to 400°F.

02

Make the Dough: Bring water, salt, and 2 tsp olive oil to a boil in a saucepan. Remove from heat and immediately stir in the flour vigorously until a stiff dough forms.

03

Shape: Transfer the hot dough into a churro-maker or piping bag with a star opening. Pipe 3-inch long lines of dough onto parchment paper and cut with shears.

04

Fry and Coat: Fry churros in batches for 3–4 minutes until golden. Drain on paper towels, then roll in the cinnamon sugar mixture while warm. Serve with chocolate sauce.

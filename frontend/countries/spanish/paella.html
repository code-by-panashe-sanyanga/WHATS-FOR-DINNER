Spain

Paella

1 HOUR PREP SERVES 4

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 5 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed or chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar and salt
  • 1 teaspoon pimentón dulce (sweet paprika)
  • A good pinch of saffron threads
  • 4 small squid, bodies sliced into rings
  • 2 cups medium-grain Spanish paella rice
  • 3 cups fish or chicken stock
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 12 jumbo shrimp in shells
  • 16 mussels, scrubbed and debearded

Instructions

01

Create the Sofrito: Fry the onion in oil until soft. Stir in garlic, tomatoes, sugar, salt, pimentón, and saffron. Cook until reduced to a jammy sauce. Add squid and rice, stirring until grains are coated.

02

Cook the Rice: Pour boiling stock and wine over the rice. Cook over low heat for 18–20 minutes without stirring. Lay shrimp on top after 10 minutes, turning when pink. Cover with foil once the rice is done.

03

Steam Mussels: Steam the mussels in a separate pan with a little water until they open. Discard any that remain closed.

04

Finish: Arrange the cooked mussels on top of the paella before serving.

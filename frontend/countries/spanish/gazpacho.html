Gazpacho

20 MINS PREP SERVES 4

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cucumber, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh herbs (tarragon, thyme, or parsley), chopped
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • Juice of 1/2 a lemon
  • Kosher salt and Cayenne pepper to taste
  • 1 cup croutons (for garnish)

Instructions

01

Reserve Garnishes: In a small bowl, reserve 2 tablespoons each of the chopped tomato, cucumber, pepper, and onion to use as a fresh garnish later.

02

Purée: Place all remaining ingredients (except the croutons) into a food processor or blender. Process until completely smooth, then adjust the seasoning with lemon juice, salt, and cayenne pepper.

03

Chill: Cover and refrigerate for at least 3 hours, though overnight is preferred for the best flavor.

04

Finish and Serve: Adjust the consistency with a little water if desired. Serve in chilled bowls, topped with the reserved vegetables and crispy croutons.

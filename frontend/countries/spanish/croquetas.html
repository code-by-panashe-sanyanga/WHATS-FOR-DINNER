Croquetas

50 MINS PREP SERVES 4

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs russet potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup fine dry bread crumbs
  • Vegetable oil for deep-frying

Instructions

01

Prepare the Mash: Boil potatoes in salted water until tender (about 15-20 mins). Drain and mash until smooth, then stir in cream, butter, egg yolks, salt, and pepper.

02

Shape the Croquetas: Spoon tablespoons of the potato mixture and roll them into small balls between your palms.

03

Bread the Balls: Roll each ball in flour, then dip in the beaten eggs, and finally coat thoroughly in bread crumbs. Chill the breaded balls for 30 minutes to help them set.

04

Fry and Keep Warm: Heat 1 1/2 inches of oil to 360°F. Fry in batches for about 1 1/2 minutes until golden brown. Drain on paper towels and keep warm in a 200°F oven until ready to serve.

Patatas Bravas

30 MINS PREP SERVES 4

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled, sliced 1/8" thick
  • 1 large white onion, thinly sliced
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups extra-virgin olive oil
  • Kosher salt to taste

Instructions

01

Prep the Potatoes: Toss potatoes with salt and let sit for 20 minutes. Pat thoroughly dry with paper towels to ensure they crisp properly in the oil.

02

Fry the Vegetables: Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high. Cook potatoes until crisp-tender (10-15 mins), then add the onion and continue cooking until both are soft. Transfer the mixture to a bowl with a slotted spoon, reserving the oil.

03

Combine and Cook: Add beaten eggs to the potato mixture and season with salt. Return 1/4 cup of the reserved oil to the skillet and add the egg mixture, arranging potatoes so they are submerged. Cook on medium until almost set (25-30 mins).

04

Flip and Finish: Invert the tortilla onto a plate, then slide it back into the skillet browned-side up. Cook for 4 more minutes until golden on the bottom and fully cooked through. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Mango Sticky Rice

20 MINS PREP SERVES 4-6

Ingredients

  • 3 cups sticky rice (soaked overnight)
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 3/4 cup palm or brown sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 ripe mangoes
  • Fresh mint or basil for garnish

Instructions

01

Steam the Rice: Drain the soaked sticky rice and steam until tender.

02

Prepare Coconut Sauce: Heat coconut milk, sugar, and salt in a pot over medium heat. Stir until dissolved, but do not let it boil.

03

Infuse the Rice: Place cooked rice in a bowl and pour 1 cup of the hot coconut milk over it. Let stand for 20-60 minutes to absorb flavors.

04

Slice & Serve: Peel and slice mangoes. Serve rice with mangoes on the side and drizzle with the remaining coconut sauce.

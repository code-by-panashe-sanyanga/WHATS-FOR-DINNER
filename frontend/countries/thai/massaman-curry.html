Massaman Curry

2.5 HOURS SERVES 6

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4–4 1/2 lb chicken pieces
  • 4 Yukon Gold potatoes
  • 2 red onions, wedged
  • 3/4 cup massaman paste
  • 12 oz wheat beer
  • 4 cans coconut milk
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • Fish sauce, lime, palm sugar
  • Cilantro & shallots for garnish

Instructions

01

Sear the Chicken: Heat oil in a heavy pot. Season chicken with salt and cook skin side down until golden brown (8–10 mins). Transfer to a plate.

02

Brown the Vegetables: In the same pot, cook potatoes until brown (8–10 mins), then onions until golden (5–8 mins). Transfer both to a plate.

03

Bloom the Paste: Stir curry paste into the pot until fragrant (4 mins). Add beer and simmer until reduced by half.

04

Slow Simmer: Add chicken, coconut milk, and broth. Simmer for 1–1.5 hours until chicken is very tender.

05

Final Finish: Return potatoes and onions to the pot for 30 minutes. Season with fish sauce, lime, and sugar. Garnish with cilantro.

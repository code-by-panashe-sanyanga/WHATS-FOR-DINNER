Thailand

Pad Thai

30 MINS SERVES 2

Ingredients

  • 5 oz rice noodles
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 6 medium shrimp (optional)
  • 2 tbsp pressed tofu slices
  • 1 tbsp sweet radish
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • Tamarind, Fish Sauce, Syrup
  • Garlic chives & peanuts

Instructions

01

Soak the Noodles: Place noodles in a bowl of hot water. Let soak for 5–10 minutes until tender but not mushy, then drain.

02

Sauté Protein: Heat oil in a wok. Scramble the egg for 30 seconds, then add shrimp and cook until almost done.

03

The Stir-Fry: Add tofu and radish. Toss in the noodles and cook for 1 minute. Stir in bean sprouts.

04

Sauce & Finish: Add tamarind, fish sauce, and syrup. Stir-fry until the sauce is absorbed. Mix in chives, chiles, and peanuts.

05

Serve: Garnish with extra peanuts, chiles, and fresh lime wedges.

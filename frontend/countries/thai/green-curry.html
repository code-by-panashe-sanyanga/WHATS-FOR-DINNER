Thailand

Green Curry

20 MINS PREP SERVES 4

Ingredients

  • 2 lemongrass stalks, tough outer layers removed
  • 8 ounces serrano chiles (12–15), seeds removed
  • 1 large shallot, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, peeled
  • 1 (3-inch) piece ginger, peeled, thinly sliced
  • 1 (2-inch) piece turmeric, peeled, chopped
  • 1 kaffir lime leaf, chopped
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp sugar

Instructions

01

Make the Paste: Finely grate the lemongrass into a blender. Add chiles, shallot, garlic, ginger, turmeric, lime leaf, and spices. Blend with a splash of water or oil until a smooth, vibrant green paste forms.

02

Fry the Aromatics: Heat 2 tbsp of oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add your fresh curry paste and fry for 2–3 minutes. This "blooms" the spices and removes the raw onion/garlic taste, making the curry much deeper in flavor.

03

Build the Base: Pour in 400ml of full-fat coconut milk and 1/2 cup of chicken or vegetable broth. Stir to dissolve the paste completely and bring to a gentle simmer (do not boil rapidly or the coconut milk may separate).

04

Simmer & Finish: Add your choice of protein (chicken or tofu) and vegetables. Simmer for 8–10 minutes until cooked through. At the very last second, stir in a handful of fresh Thai basil and a squeeze of lime juice for brightness.

Coq au Vin

<

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle (750ml) dry red wine
  • 1.5kg chicken, cut into 8 pieces
  • 200g bacon lardons
  • 250g mushrooms, halved
  • 2 carrots, thickly sliced
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • Fresh thyme and rosemary sprigs

Instructions

01

Browning: Season chicken with salt and pepper. In a large heavy-based pot, brown the chicken in batches until golden. Transfer to a plate.

02

Aromatics: Add bacon lardons to the pot and cook until rendered. Add carrots, celery, and onion; cook until translucent (about 7-8 minutes). Stir in 1 cup of wine and tomato paste.

03

Braise: Add the remaining wine and broth. Tie thyme and rosemary sprigs together and add to the pot. Cover and transfer to the oven to braise at 160°C until tender, about 1 1/4 hours.

04

Finish: Sauté mushrooms in a separate pan until browned. Add them to the reduced sauce along with the chicken. Season with salt and pepper to taste before serving.

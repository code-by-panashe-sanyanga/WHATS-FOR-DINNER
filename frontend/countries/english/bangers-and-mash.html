Bangers and Mash

10 MINS PREP SERVES 4

Ingredients

  • 2 medium fennel bulbs, fronds reserved, bulbs sliced
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 sprigs rosemary
  • 4 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper
  • 7 sweet Italian sausages (about 1 1/2 lb. total)
  • 1 medium navel orange
  • 1 Tbsp. red wine vinegar
  • Flaky sea salt

Instructions

01

Prep and Roast: Preheat oven to 425°F. Toss fennel, onion, and rosemary on a baking sheet with oil and seasoning. Arrange sausages on top, prick with a knife, and roast for 25–30 minutes until browned.

02

Prepare Toppings: Chop reserved fennel fronds. Peel and segment the orange over a bowl to catch the juice. Mix orange segments and juice with vinegar, salt, and pepper.

03

Deglaze: Pour the orange juices over the hot sausages and fennel straight from the oven. Let the mixture sit and cool for about 5 minutes.

04

Garnish & Serve: Transfer to a platter. Scatter the rosemary, orange segments, and fennel fronds on top. Finish with a drizzle of oil and flaky sea salt.

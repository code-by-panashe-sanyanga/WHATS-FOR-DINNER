Chicken Tikka Masala

25 MINS PREP SERVES 4

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup plain whole-milk Greek-style yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons peanut oil
  • 2 teaspoons fresh lime or lemon juice
  • 1 large clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne
  • 1 tablespoon grated peeled fresh ginger
  • 4 tablespoons (1/2 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1 large white onion, finely chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups canned tomato purée
  • 3/4 cup water

Instructions

01

Prepare the Chicken: Prick the chicken breasts all over with a fork. Arrange between sheets of plastic wrap and beat to an even 1/2- to 3/4-inch thickness. Whisk yogurt, 1 tbsp oil, lime juice, and garlic; rub the marinade over the chicken and set aside.

02

Make the Sauce: Whisk together the dry spices and ginger. Melt butter in a sauté pan over medium-high heat and sauté the onion until caramelized, about 5 minutes. Stir in the spices, tomato purée, water, and cream, then simmer until thickened.

03

Cook the Chicken: Heat a skillet with 1/2 tbsp peanut oil over moderately high heat. Cook chicken in batches for 6–8 minutes total until browned and cooked through. Transfer to a cutting board and slice into 1 1/2-inch pieces.

04

Simmer and Finish: Add the sliced chicken to the simmering sauce and cook for 5 more minutes. Stir in pepper and chopped cilantro. Garnish with additional cilantro and serve with naan or rice.

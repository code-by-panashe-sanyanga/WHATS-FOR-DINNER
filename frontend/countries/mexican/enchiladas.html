Mexico

Enchiladas

45 MINS PREP SERVES 4

Ingredients

  • 1 rotisserie chicken
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup crème fraîche
  • 1/2 cup crumbled queso fresco
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup salsa roja or salsa verde
  • 1/2 small white onion, thinly sliced
  • Kosher salt to taste

Instructions

01

Prepare the Chicken: Pull meat from the rotisserie chicken and shred it. Reheat the shredded meat in a pan with just enough broth to cover, seasoning with salt as needed.

02

Prepare Toppings: In a small bowl, mix the crème fraîche and crumbled queso fresco until combined.

03

Soften Tortillas: Heat oil in a skillet and submerge each tortilla for 5–10 seconds to soften. Drain on paper towels.

04

Assemble: Fill each tortilla with 1/3 cup of shredded chicken, roll tightly, and place seam-side down. Ladle your chosen sauce over the top and garnish with the cheese mixture and sliced onions.

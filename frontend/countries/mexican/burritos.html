Mexico

Burritos

30 MINS PREP SERVES 4

Ingredients

  • 500g Minced Beef
  • 400g Pinto or Black beans
  • 1.5 Cups white rice
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • Canola oil spray, oil for the pan
  • 8 corn tortillas, warmed if desired
  • Sour cream, cheese or avocado (optional)

Instructions

01

Cook and season meat: Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.Add your oil and Crumble ground beef into the hot skillet and cook for 3-5 minutes or until the meat is no longer red. Drain or spoon out all the excess oil. Season with paprika, cumin, onion, garlic and chili powder. Set aside.

02

Cook Rice: Wash Rice 2-3 times under cold water to get rid of starch, then fill the pot with clean cold water, add salt and a drizzle of oil. Turn the heat on high until the water starts to bubble, then stir and turn the heat to simmer, low temperature. Cover and allow it to simmer for 8 minutes. Turn the heat off, and remove the pot from the heat. Do not take the lid off. Allow it to steam for 5 more minutes under the lid.

03

Add Fillings: Add your chpice of beans and salsa to the pan. Continue to cook for another 2 minutes or until all ingredients are heated through.

04

Assemble and Serve: Add a healthy serving or your seasoned minced meat onto the tortilla. Add slightly less rice then meat. Add optional sour cream, cheese, or avocado, then roll or fold in half to serve.

Mexico

Tacos

20 MINS PREP SERVES 4

Ingredients

  • 12 taco shells
  • 2 cans (20 oz each) young green jackfruit in brine, drained and rinsed
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup vegetable stock
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons Tabasco sauce
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 2 limes
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Guacamole and salsa for serving

Instructions

01

Prep the Jackfruit: Chop the jackfruit into small pieces and sauté with the red onion in a large pan until the onion is soft and translucent.

02

Simmer: Add the maple syrup, vegetable stock, Tabasco, and the juice of half a lime. Cover and simmer for 7–10 minutes until the liquid is fully absorbed.

03

Season: Remove the lid and sprinkle in the cumin, smoked paprika, chili powder, and salt. Stir until the jackfruit is well coated and takes on the rich color of the spices.

04

Assemble and Serve: Serve the warm spiced jackfruit in taco shells topped with guacamole, fresh salsa, cilantro leaves, and extra lime wedges.

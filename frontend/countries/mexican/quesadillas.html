Mexico

Quesadillas

15 MINS PREP SERVES 2

Ingredients

  • 2 tortillas
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (Monterey Jack or Cheddar)
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms

Instructions

01

Heat the Tortilla: Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Place the tortilla in the pan and cook for about 1 minute until it begins to bubble.

02

Add Cheese: Flip the tortilla over and sprinkle the grated cheese evenly across the top.

03

Add Filling: Once the cheese begins to melt, place the sliced mushrooms on one half of the tortilla and fold it in half.

04

Finish and Serve: Cook for 2 minutes on each side until the cheese is fully melted and the tortilla is hot. Remove from heat and add any extra stuffings as desired.

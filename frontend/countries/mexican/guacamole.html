Guacamole

10 MINS PREP SERVES 4

Ingredients

  • 3 large avocados
  • 1 cup kale leaves, stems removed
  • 2 plum tomatoes, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup minced red onion
  • 2 jalapeño chilies, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, minced

Instructions

01

Prep the Kale: Place the kale leaves in a food processor and pulse until they are finely chopped.

02

Mash Avocados: Remove the pits from the avocados and scoop the flesh into a large bowl. Add salt and mash with a wooden spoon until you reach your desired texture.

03

Mix Ingredients: Stir in the chopped kale, tomatoes, red onion, jalapeños, lime juice, and cilantro. Taste and adjust salt as needed.

04

Store and Serve: If not serving immediately, place plastic wrap directly on the surface of the dip to prevent browning and refrigerate. Serve with tortilla chips or pita wedges.

Mexico

Nachos

15 MINS PREP SERVES 4

Ingredients

  • 1 bag tortilla chips (blue or white corn)
  • 2 cups Mexican blend cheese (Monterey Jack, Cheddar, Queso Blanco)
  • 15 oz can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • Guacamole and salsa
  • Sour cream
  • Sliced black olives (optional)

Instructions

01

Prep and Layer: Preheat your oven to 350°F. Place half of the tortilla chips in an oven-safe dish or on a cookie sheet.

02

First Toppings: Cover the first layer of chips with half of the cheese and half of the black beans.

03

Final Layer and Bake: Repeat the process with the remaining chips, cheese, and beans. Bake for 10–15 minutes until the cheese is completely melted over the chips.

04

Garnish: Finish the dish by topping with guacamole, sour cream, and fresh salsa. Add sliced black olives if desired.

Peking Duck

2 HOURS PREP SERVES 4

Ingredients

  • 1 whole duck (4–5 lbs)
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp five-spice powder
  • For serving: Pancakes, scallions, cucumbers, hoisin sauce

Instructions

01

Clean the duck and pat it extremely dry. Use poultry scissors to remove excess fat and the wings if desired.

02

Combine honey, soy sauce, and five-spice powder. Brush the glaze all over the duck skin. Let the duck rest uncovered in the fridge for at least 4 hours to dry the skin.

03

Roast the duck at 350°F (175°C) on a rack over a pan for about 1 hour and 15 minutes. Increase heat for the last 10 minutes to crisp the skin.

04

Carve the crispy skin and meat. Serve with warm pancakes, hoisin sauce, and julienned scallions and cucumbers.

Sichuan Hot Pot

90 MINS PREP SERVES 4

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef chuck or brisket
  • 2 tbsp chili bean paste
  • 3 star anise pods
  • 1 tsp Sichuan peppercorns
  • 1 inch ginger, sliced
  • 6 cups beef broth
  • 1 lb wheat noodles
  • Bok choy and scallions
  • Cilantro for garnish

Instructions

01

In a large pot, sear the beef until browned. Add the chili bean paste, ginger, star anise, and Sichuan peppercorns. Stir for 1 minute until fragrant.

02

Pour in the beef broth. Simmer for 45–60 minutes until the meat is very tender. Adjust seasoning with soy sauce or salt if needed.

03

Cook wheat noodles in a separate pot according to package directions. Drain well and divide into bowls.

04

Ladle the hot soup and meat over the noodles. Garnish with scallions and cilantro. Serve with bok choy if desired.

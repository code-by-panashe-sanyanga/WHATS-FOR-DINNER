Sweet and Sour Pork

45 MINS PREP SERVES 4

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork shoulder, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 cups vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1/2 onion, cut into chunks
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • Sauce: 1/3 cup vinegar, 1/4 cup sugar, 2 tbsp ketchup, 1 tbsp soy sauce

Instructions

01

In a medium bowl, combine the pork, cornstarch, and egg. Use your hands to thoroughly coat the pork with the batter. Let sit for 10 to 15 minutes.

02

In a small bowl, combine the vinegar, sugar, ketchup, and soy sauce. Stir until the sugar is dissolved and set aside.

03

Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Working in batches, add the pork and fry until golden-brown on the outside and cooked through, 4 to 5 minutes. Remove and drain on paper towels.

04

Wipe the wok and heat 1 tbsp oil. Add garlic and ginger, stir-frying for 20 seconds. Add the pineapple, peppers, onion, and the sauce. Simmer for 2–3 minutes.

05

Return the pork to the wok and toss until well coated with the sauce. Transfer to a plate and serve immediately.

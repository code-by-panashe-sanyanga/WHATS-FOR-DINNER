Kung Pao Chicken

30 MINS PREP SERVES 2

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cubed
  • 1/2 cup roasted peanuts
  • 6–8 dried red chilies
  • 1 tsp Sichuan peppercorns
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • Sauce: 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp black vinegar, 1 tsp sugar, 1 tsp cornstarch

Instructions

01

Marinate the chicken with a little soy sauce and cornstarch. Whisk all sauce ingredients together in a separate bowl.

02

Heat oil in a wok. Add dried chilies and Sichuan peppercorns, frying until fragrant and slightly blackened.

03

Add chicken and stir-fry until no longer pink. Stir in the garlic and ginger for 30 seconds.

04

Pour in the sauce and toss to coat. Stir in the peanuts and cook for another minute until the sauce thickens. Serve immediately.

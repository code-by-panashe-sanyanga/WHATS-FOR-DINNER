Spring Rolls

30 MINS PREP SERVES 4

Ingredients

  • 10–12 spring roll wrappers
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 2 scallions, chopped
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 4 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

01

Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a skillet. Add the cabbage and carrots and saute for 5 minutes, until tender.

02

Add the scallions and salt and saute an additional 3 minutes. Stir in soy sauce, vinegar, and sesame oil to coat.

03

Place a wrapper in a diamond shape. Put 2 tbsp filling in the center. Fold corners inward, brush with water, and roll up tightly.

04

Heat remaining oil in the skillet. Cook spring rolls in batches for 2–5 minutes per side until golden brown and crispy.

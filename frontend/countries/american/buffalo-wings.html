American

Buffalo Wings

45 MINS PREP SERVES 4

Ingredients

  • 4 large carrots, peeled
  • 4 large ribs celery, trimmed
  • 4 pounds chicken wings (about 24), tips removed
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons Tabasco sauce
  • 1 cup peanut oil
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Danish Blue-Cheese Dip

Instructions

01

Prep Vegetables: Cut the carrots and celery into sticks; set aside.

02

Prep Chicken: Using a sharp knife, separate the chicken wings at the joint so you have a "drumstick" and a "thigh." Rinse and pat dry, then set aside.

03

Sauce Base: Melt the butter with the Tabasco sauce in a small saucepan. Transfer it to a large bowl and set aside.

04

Fry & Toss: Heat the peanut and vegetable oils in a deep, heavy pot over medium-high heat. When the oils are hot, cook the chicken in batches until golden brown and cooked through, about 10 minutes. Remove with tongs and drain on paper towels. Place in the bowl and toss with the Tabasco mixture to coat. Season with salt and pepper.

05

Serve: Serve the wings immediately with the carrot and celery sticks and blue-cheese dip alongside.

Miso Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 shallot, chopped
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 cups sliced bok choy
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1 cup edamame, shelled
  • 12 wonton wrappers
  • 3 tablespoons white miso, plus more to taste
  • 2 scallions
  • thinly sliced

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 375°F.
  2. Prick holes in potatoes.
  3. Bake on a baking sheet until soft, turning once, about 1 hour.
  4. Cool, then peel and mash.
  5. Heat oil in a small skillet over medium heat.
  6. Cook garlic and shallot, stirring, until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes.
  7. Add pepper flakes.
  8. Stir garlic mixture, salt and pepper into potatoes.
  9. Place bok choy, sprouts and edamame in a pot with 2 cups water and set aside.
  10. Lay 1 wonton wrapper in palm of hand.
